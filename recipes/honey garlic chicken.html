Honey Garlic Chicken


This Honey Garlic Chicken recipe is the perfect dinner for a busy weeknight! You can make this recipe with simple pantry staples and one pan in less than 30 minutes. The sweet and savory sauce is so irresistible.

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ⅓ cup honey
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar or apple cider vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • Sesame seeds
  • Sliced green onion

STEPS

  1. Season the chicken pieces all over with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Place the flour in a shallow dish.
  2. In a small mixing bowl, whisk together the honey, soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic cloves.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the oil over medium-high heat. Coat the chicken in flour and place directly into the hot skillet. Cook until browned, about 5 minutes. Flip and brown on the other side, about 5 minutes more.
  4. Pour the sauce into the skillet and shake it to distribute throughout the pan. (It may bubble up quite a bit.) Turn the heat down to medium-low. Simmer for 3 minutes. Flip the chicken, coating the pieces in the sauce. Cook for another few minutes or until the chicken is cooked through. (If the sauce gets too thick, add a splash of water to thin it out.) Serve immediately and garnish with sliced green onion and sesame seeds, if desired.
